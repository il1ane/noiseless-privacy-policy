Noiseless

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2026

Noiseless helps you hide distracting sections on social sites in Safari while keeping your data private.

Data We Store

  • Your filter preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Extension settings are shared with Safari using Apple's app group storage.
  • If cloud sync is enabled by the user, settings may sync through the user's iCloud account.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No browsing history collection.
  • No tracking pixels or advertising identifiers.
  • No account passwords, page content, or personal messages.

Payments

If paid features are offered, billing is handled directly by Apple through the App Store.

Your Controls

  • You can disable the extension anytime in Safari Settings.
  • You can reset app settings by removing and reinstalling the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact lessnoisetools@icloud.com.
