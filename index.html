Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 27, 2026

Veil is designed to help you reduce distractions in Safari while keeping your data private.

What We Store

  • Veil stores your app settings on your device so Safari can apply them.
  • If you enable Premium sync, your settings are synced via your own iCloud account.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your browsing history.
  • We do not collect website content.
  • We do not collect passwords.

Payments

Subscriptions and payments are processed by Apple through StoreKit.

Your Control

You can disable sync at any time in the app Settings.
